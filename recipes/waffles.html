Waffles

This is the easiest homemade waffle recipe you can make with simple ingredients found in your pantry. They are quick to prepare and they'll be your family favourite for years to come.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all purpose Flour
  • 2 tbs Sugar
  • 1 Egg beaten
  • 2 tbs Oil
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Essence

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients together in a large mixing bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl add all wet ingredients and mix slightly.
  3. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and combine. Do not overmix.
  4. Ladle the mixture into a waffle iron and cook till golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with syrup, cream and ice-cream or toppings of your choice.
