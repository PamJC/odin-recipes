Waffles

Homemade waffles

This is the easiest homemade waffle recipe you can make with simple ingredients found in your pantry. They are quick to prepare and they'll be your family favourite for years to come.

Ingredients

    Flour Sugar Eggs Oil Milk Baking Powder Salt Vanilla Essence

Steps

    Mix dry ingredients together in a large mixing bowl In a separate bowl add all wet ingredients and mix slightly Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and combine. Do not overmix.
