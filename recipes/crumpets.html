Crumpets

This is the easiest homemade crumpet recipe you can make with simple ingredients found in your pantry. They are quick to prepare and they'll be your family favourite for years to come.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 cups self-raising flour
  • Oil for pan frying
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 1 tsp Bicarbonate of soda
  • 1 tsp Salt

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients together in a large mixing bowl
  2. In a separate bowl add all wet ingredients and mix slightly
  3. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and combine. Do not overmix.
  4. Heat a small amount of oil in a pan and drop spoonfuls of the mixture into hot oil.
  5. Flip crumpets when bubbles appear on the surface. Cook till golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with butter, jam, cream and grated cheddar cheese.
